Curriculum

What Will You Study?

Course 1: Foundations of Business Psychology — built through experiential learning in coaching, facilitation, design thinking, personal branding, wellbeing and psychometric applications.

01
Essentials of Business Psychology

A comprehensive introduction to applying psychological principles in organisational settings — motivation, communication, decision-making, culture, leadership styles and employee wellbeing.

02
Facilitation Skills

Develop the competencies to guide group interactions and collaborative activities — active listening, group dynamics, problem-solving and leading inclusive, productive sessions.

03
Coaching in Action

Core coaching principles and practice — active listening, powerful questioning, constructive feedback and goal-setting, applied to confidence, resilience and behavioural change.

04
Design Thinking & Business Strategy Planning

Combines design thinking — empathise, define, ideate, prototype, test — with strategic planning that aligns innovation with long-term business objectives.

05
Personal Branding & Digital Marketing

Build a strong professional identity and communicate your value online — social media engagement, content creation, audience targeting and authentic personal branding.

06
Hypnotherapy in Mental Well-being

3 hours of learning plus 2 hours of practice to identify and manage stress, develop strategies to perform better at work and become a stronger decision-maker.

07
Role of Psychometrics in HR

From the foundations of psychometrics through personality and cognitive ability testing to strengths-based development and Situational Judgment Tests, with a hands-on practical session.

08
Psychometric Immersion: Lumina Learning

5 hours combining theory with hands-on practice of the Lumina Learning tool for self-awareness, team working and behavioural dynamics.

09
Wrap-Up Session & Way Forward: Q&A

A highly interactive 5-hour live, in-person session in Bengaluru to synthesise concepts and arrive at actionable methods for career advancement.

10
One-on-One Coaching on Way Forward

2 hours of individual coaching per participant to develop and execute a concrete action plan tied to your own career goals.

FAQs

Everything you wanted to ask.

How is this different from a Master's, diploma or specialist certification?⌄

Most programmes focus on a single discipline within Psychology or HR. PeopleProcess offers a broader perspective — coaching, leadership, assessment, organisational development, wellbeing and workplace behaviour — so you understand the full landscape before specialising further.

I'm not a Psychology student. Can I still benefit?⌄

Absolutely. The programme is designed for students, HR professionals, practitioners and working professionals from diverse backgrounds. No prior Psychology qualification is required.

Will this help me decide on a future specialisation or career path?⌄

Yes. A key objective is helping you gain clarity about your interests, strengths and potential career pathways, by learning directly from specialists across different domains.

What makes this programme unique?⌄

Rather than one instructor covering one niche, you learn from a curated faculty of 11 international specialists, each bringing deep expertise from their own field — within a single programme.

What will I walk away with?⌄

A professional certification, plus a comprehensive understanding of Business Psychology and its integration with HR, exposure to global best practices, valuable professional connections, and clarity on where to focus your future learning.

Curriculum

What Will You Study?

Course 1: Foundations of Business Psychology — ten modules engineered for the real workplace.

01
The Psychology Behind Workplace Motivation

Move past carrots and sticks. Explore intrinsic drivers, Self-Determination Theory, and how to design work that people actually want to do.

02
Cognitive Biases in Business Decisions

Identify anchoring, confirmation, and sunk-cost traps that quietly derail hiring, strategy, and product decisions — and build debiasing rituals.

03
Building High-Performance Teams

Apply Tuckman, Lencioni, and Google's Project Aristotle findings to compose, launch, and sustain teams that consistently outperform.

04
Emotional Intelligence for Leaders

Train the four EQ domains — self-awareness, self-management, social awareness, relationship management — with practical, on-the-job drills.

05
Organisational Culture & Change

Diagnose existing culture using Schein's model and lead change without the resistance, burnout, or quiet quitting that usually follows.

06
The Science of Persuasion & Influence

Master Cialdini's seven principles and modern behavioural-economics nudges to influence ethically across stakeholders and customers.

07
Conflict Resolution & Negotiation Psychology

Use interest-based bargaining, tactical empathy, and BATNA analysis to convert workplace conflict into clear, durable agreements.

08
Consumer Behaviour & Decision Making

Understand how customers actually buy — heuristics, framing, loss aversion — and apply it to product, marketing, and CX decisions.

09
Psychological Safety in the Workplace

Drawing on Amy Edmondson's research, install the everyday behaviours that let teams speak up, take risks, and innovate without fear.

10
Applied Psychology: Capstone Workshop

Bring it all together: a live, mentor-reviewed project applying psychological principles to a real workplace challenge of your choice.

FAQs

Everything you wanted to ask.

Do I need a psychology background to join?⌄

Not at all. PeopleProcess is built for non-psychology backgrounds — engineers, MBAs, founders, marketers. We translate research into practical, workplace-ready frameworks.

How much time should I budget per week?⌄

Plan for 4–6 hours weekly: one live session, one reading sprint, and one applied assignment. The programme is designed to fit alongside a full-time role.

Are sessions live or recorded?⌄

Both. Live sessions happen every Saturday and are recorded the same day. You'll never lose progress if you miss one — and recordings stay accessible for 12 months.

Is the certificate industry-recognised?⌄

Yes. The MenteSquare certificate is verified, shareable on LinkedIn, and accepted by partner organisations across India's HR, consulting, and startup ecosystems.

What is your refund policy?⌄

Try the first two weeks risk-free. If PeopleProcess isn't for you, request a full refund before the end of Week 2 — no questions asked.

Do you offer EMI options?⌄

Yes. EMIs are available through Razorpay across major Indian credit and debit cards, with no-cost EMI promotions on select banks during enrollment windows.

Will I get placement support after the course?⌄

Practitioner and Leader tiers include access to MenteSquare's hiring partner network, profile reviews, and warm intros for HR, L&D, and people-ops roles.
